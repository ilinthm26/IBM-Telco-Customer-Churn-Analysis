# projects
1️⃣ Background

  The telecom company has observed increasing customer attrition over the past year. Customer churn directly impacts revenue and increases acquisition costs.
  
  The management wants to understand:
  
  Why customers are leaving
  
  Which customer segments are at high risk
  
  What business strategies can reduce churn

2️⃣ Problem Statement

  The company is experiencing high churn rates.
  The objective is to analyze customer data and identify:
  
  Key drivers of churn
  
  High-risk customer segments
  
  Revenue impact of churn
  
  Actionable retention strategies

3️⃣ Business Objectives

  Calculate overall churn rate
  
  Identify churn rate by:
  
  Contract type
  
  Payment method
  
  Internet service
  
  Tenure group
  
  Monthly charges
  
  Identify revenue loss due to churn
  
  Recommend retention strategies

4️⃣ Key Business Questions

  What is the overall churn percentage?
  
  Do customers with month-to-month contracts churn more?
  
  Does higher monthly charge increase churn probability?
  
  Are new customers more likely to churn?
  
  Which services increase retention?
  
  What is the estimated monthly revenue loss due to churn?

5️⃣ KPIs to Measure

  Churn Rate (%)
  
  Average Monthly Charges (Churn vs Non-Churn)
  
  Average Tenure (Churn vs Non-Churn)
  
  Revenue at Risk
  
  High-Risk Segment Identification

6️⃣ Data Scope

  Dataset: Telco Customer Churn
  Records: ~7,000 customers
  Features:
  
  Demographics
  
  Subscription details
  
  Billing info
  
  Churn label

7️⃣ Expected Deliverables

  Data Cleaning Report
  
  Exploratory Data Analysis
  
  Visualizations
  
  Business Insights
  
  Strategic Recommendations
  
  Executive Summary
